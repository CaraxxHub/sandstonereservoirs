Frequently Asked Questions

When will 2026 registration open?

We will announce dates and open registration in early 2026. Contact us to receive updates when available.

Can I attend if I'm not currently enrolled in a PhD program?

Yes! The School welcomes postdoctoral researchers, industry professionals, and master's students with strong backgrounds in geosciences.

Is financial support available?

Partial fee waivers may be available for participants from underrepresented regions or institutions. Details will be provided when registration opens.

What is the selection process?

Applications are reviewed based on CV quality, motivation statement, relevance to career/research goals, and commitment to global representation.

Are past lecture materials available?

We are currently building a resource archive. Check back soon or contact us for specific requests.
